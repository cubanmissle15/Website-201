Formal Education Timeline

Foothill High School (2016-2019)
In my four years in High school, I was a student-athlete and played baseball while trying to maintain a high GPA of 4.0. I was a four-year letterman in baseball and received a list of achievements: Helped the Knights to three league titles (2016, 2018, 2019), 2019 All-CIF Division I Southern Section First-Team, 2019 Orange County Register All-Count, 2019 Connie Mack Golden Glove, 2019 Connie Mack All-Tournament Team. in addition to all my achievements in baseball I was also an excellent student being a part of the school Honor Roll all four years. I graduated with a 4.1 GPA and college credits for my business degree.

University of Nevada Las Vegas (2019- 2021)
I committed to UNLV in the year 2017 to play baseball and further my academic career. Here at UNLV, I decided to take the route of business with an emphasis on Finance. After two full years at UNLV, I finished with a GPA of 3.93 and multiple dean list awards achieving a GPA of 3.9 or above. In addition to this, I was a SACC representative for the baseball team for one year. On the Athletic side of the school, I received the 2020 Mountain West Conference All-Academic Team award.

Brigham Young University (2021 - Present)
After having to go through a long summer I decided to transfer to BYU and continue my education and baseball career. This led to me being able to join a better business program and overall environment. In my one semester at BYU, I finished with a 3.9 GPA and have been able to join the Finance Honor Society. This has led to connectin in the Business world that will change my life in the future.
